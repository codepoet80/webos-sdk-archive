Headers and Footers


Headers

Headers may be fixed or scrolling.

Fixed headers stay above scrolling lists, and do not move as you scroll.

Scrolling headers scroll along with the content.


Footers

Footers usually contain action buttons and are usually fixed to the bottom of the screen.


Custom, Transient Headers and Footers

Transient headers and footers usually appear temporarily above media content. They usually appear when you enter a fullscreen media experience and disappear after a timeout. They are shown again when the user taps the screen to reveal media controls and the floating header. Please note that Enyo does not currently support transient header or footer toolbars in the framework. These are custom components.



Floating headers usually contain navigation, showing you what are looking at and providing a means to return to the previous screen, which usually contains a list or grid of other content. Floating headers might also contain action buttons or other controls.



Floating footers are generally for actions and controls related to the content you are consuming or editing.
